About Us

Creating The Highest Product Quality and Image Quality with State-of-the-art Technology

Golden Toner is one of the largest toner manufacturers supporting the aftermarket. For over 40 years, Golden Toner has continually contributed to the aftermarket by supplying high quality, reliable toner products that meet and exceed the expectations of its key players.

Golden Toner ‘s unique and advanced particle shaping technology, use of high performance raw materials and commitment to quality and the environment make our toners the reliable, environmentally conscious and affordable quality choice for end users.

Golden Toner is continually committed to meet the ever changing requirements of the aftermarket by bringing smart colour technology to our door step.

With state of the art production facilities located in Japan and USA, and a comprehensive own distribution network conveniently located in Japan, USA and Europe. Golden Toner group is committed to just-in-time delivery service around the world.

Golden Toner EU TRADING SRL

In February 2004, Golden Toner group opens its own European distribution centre in the modern and cosmopolitan city of Barcelona, Spain.

Today Golden Toner EU TRADING SRL serves every customer in Europe, Middle East and Africa by offering on time product delivery and technical support.

Most products are stocked off the shelf in the Barcelona warehouse, which is located close to the port. Golden Toner ensures at all time that products are safely handled and ready to be shipped upon customer requirement.
